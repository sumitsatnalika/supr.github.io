How we met

Our story began with our parents 'arranging' for us to meet and fall in love. And luckily for us they did because what followed from there has been a beautiful story which is translating into a lifetime of togetherness for us.
Sumit has always been a self reliant, independent person who is always looking out for his people. He loves to play sports, watch movies, meet friends, learn new skills everyday. On the contrary, Priya is a big foodie, loves to travel and has a great sense of fashion. She treats everyone with respect and kindness, values friendship.
While we are in different job sectors, both of us are highly similar in nature and that is what makes us the best combination. We have discovered that we compliment and balance each other in the most beautiful manner.
We are having the most wonderful courtship filled with surprises and beautiful memories. We can't wait to start our lives together and look forward to celebrating the beginning of this on our wedding day with you all!
